About Us

Square Meter Fabrications Limited (SMF)

Square Meter Fabrications Limited (SMF) is a private limited company incorporated by youthful Zambians in the friendly city of Ndola, Copperbelt Province. The company has been serving upper-end residential, light industrial, and commercial markets since 2020.

Our commitment is to maintain personal relationships with clients while ensuring that each project receives the highest level of professionalism and attention.

We specialize in custom fabrication solutions that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal, transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments.
